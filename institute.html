About Our Institute

JSS Academy of Technical Education, Noida (JSSATEN) is one of the leading Technical Institutions in Delhi-NCR. Established in the year 1998 by JSS Mahavidyapeetha, Ghaziabad. The Institution has set benchmarks every year, and grown into an Institution of Excellence in Technical Education.

Our Departments

Department of Computer Science

Specializing in software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. Our department prepares students for cutting-edge careers in tech.

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Focuses on mechanical design, automation, thermodynamics, and materials science. Our graduates excel in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.

Department of Civil Engineering

Dedicated to infrastructure development, urban planning, and sustainable construction practices. We shape the cities of the future.

Department of Electrical Engineering

Covers areas like electrical systems, renewable energy, telecommunications, and embedded systems. Our engineers are leaders in innovation.

Department of Biotechnology

Blends biological sciences with technology to advance healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability through innovative research.

Department of Business Administration

Offers a comprehensive curriculum in management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship to develop future business leaders.
